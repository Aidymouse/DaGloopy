# The Riddle of Steel X

Around the corner The Book find an abandoned campfire. In a cavern nearby they discover the women and children of the fish people. **The Fiend** and **Simon** slaughter them without reprove. **The Crouton** and **The Warden** do not protest, but leave the cavern rather than witness the killing. **Ganent** has stayed with the raft, and is much better off not knowing about the actions of his new compatriots, for he would surely not approve.

The Book return to the raft and set sail for home. The journey is easier now that the fog has lifted. And though the rowing is easier, the weight missing from the raft is anything but comforting.

After their arrival home, **The Fiend** announces the success of the expedition and decrees that all in the cave must show their loyalty to their new leader (I believe the exact words were "join me if you want to live!"). Unfortunately for him, the admiral **Kushiria** has woken up. An argument between **The Fiend** and **Gregor** about who is to rule is put to rest by **Ganent**, and The Book rest. Several of their number attain third level, with **Ganent** and **Simon** attain second.

The next day, The Admiral decrees his position of power over the camp, though he is indisposed. The Book accept his command, though some are more skeptical than others. The Admiral declares the next course of action to be finding the other survivors, but The Book convince him that they would be better served getting a better look at the lay of the land. The Admiral agrees, letting the book venture out while he gathers a crew to man the ship recovered from The Sword.

The Book leave the cave through the aerie and return to the monastery, finding it free of snakemen. They then journey northwest through the wilderness, adding to their map.

One night, **Ganent** keeps watch from behind their vine-curtained overhang. He hears noises and pokes his head out the curtain. He sees a bipedal lion-headed creature stepping through the woods. Though it stops to sniff, it does not spot them.

A few nights later, The Book rest in a tree when they are suddenly set upon by 3 displacer beasts! During the fight, **The Crouton** receives a vicious wound. **Ganent** cries out "No! Stay with us!" and feels a familiar sense of hope well up inside him. Before his eyes, **The Croutons** wounds begin to heal! The recent boost in their magical power proves a great boon, and though there are some close calls, but The Book manage to survive.
