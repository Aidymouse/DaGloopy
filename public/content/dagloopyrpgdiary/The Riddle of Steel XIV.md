# The Riddle of Steel XIV

The Book return to camp. Warped as they are, **Ganent** and **The Crouton** enter the cave first. The villagers have set up a fortified camp further into the cave. Here the pair are met by **Gregor**, the physician and regroup with **Bunsen**. They return to The Book and look over their magical curses but the doctor can do nothing for them. The enter the camp and wake The Admiral, **Cuchurella**. They share some brief intel, and it comes to light that someone has spilled the beans about The Books powers. The Admiral takes a very firm stance, banning the use of magic in the camp for fear it might corrupt others.

The Book then take a long-needed rest.

**Simon** trawls the beach for materials to make crossbow bolts. **Ganent** tags along, and the pair have a chat about faith. **Ganent** is unable to coaxe out **Simons** reason for coming to the new land. **Simon** secretly finds 7 eggs, and hides them in camp, intending to nurture them.

**The Enmity** and **The Crouton** also search the beach. The find a cracked telescope, which **The Crouton** repairs with a mending spell. The duo discuss the admirals influence and how they must play their cards right, but not necessarily stay completely loyal to him.

**The Fiend** investigates his magic seashell, but all he gets is a bad feeling about trying to identify its purpose.

**The Gelid** finds **Vomert**, who is suspected, and very quickly confirmed, to be the one who told The Admiral about the books magic. Though **The Gelid** believed there to be a friendship between he and **Vomert**, it's more one sided than it seems. The conversation ends with a threat on **Vomerts** life if he ever spreads secrets again.

**The Warden** also searches the beach, and finds nothing but a porous rock.

Before meeting with The Admiral again, The Book have a tense discussion about what to say. Some see no other option but to work with him, but some argue there's no point in settling a new land only to fall under the same powers as at home. The settle on doing what he asks for now, but are worried they'll be exiled after their usefulness runs dry - when the survivors are found.

The Books journey is shared in full with The Admiral. He wants to send them north, in an upgraded raft. An expedition went north 6 days ago and hasn't returned, so The Book are tasked with trying to find them. They set sail the next morning.

After a day of mapping out the northern coast, **The Crouton** spots a derelict vessel floating empty in the water. It's the other expedition! Their boat is empty and there are signs of a small struggle. While investigating, a column of smoke is spotted on the nearby beach. The Book make haste there, finding a great bonfire. Snake men are dancing and slithering around it. A black cloud of smoke coalesces in the air above the fire. Just as The Book get close, the snake men flee into the forest and their cloud comes with them. The Book pursue them to a clearing where they find the previous expedition locked in a cage!

**The Warden** starts the battle, launching a spell from the trees. Serious blows are landed against **The Enmity** and **Ganent** from the snake mens weapons and one snakemans magic. The black cloud drips black rain onto the prisoners, who howl in pain. Eventually, though, the fight is won and the prisoners freed. The snake sorcerer and some of her minions escape into the forest.
