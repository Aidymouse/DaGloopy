# The Kinds of Games I Have Run

Know thyself, Creatures.

from Occluse RPG and Dramatic Combat
"All things being equal, I actually like D&D style role playing games. I like one figurehead who's in charge of the setting, and I like that players explore that space, rather than having greats amount of input. Games of this ilk often have binary pass/fail mechanics as they fall more in line with this style of authoratative god figure taken on by the GM."
