
# I have decided I don't not like Blades in the Dark.

Greetings from Doskvol, creatures.


I was going to write an article about how, after grappling with it for so long, I have given up on trying to enjoy blades in the dark. My main argument was that pretending to do a heist is not the same as doing a heist. That is, BitD does a really good job of telling stories about thieves who have heisted, but doesn't make you feel like you're in a heist.

But then I played a heist for real, in a different system, and I started to understand whyblades does it that way.

And then we ran a heist in masks, another PBTA game, and something clicked into place.
