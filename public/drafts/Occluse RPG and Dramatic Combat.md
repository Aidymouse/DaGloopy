## The State of the Game

Ever since [Worldwide RPG]() I've had this concept swimming around in my head. I have been distracted for a little while trying to get a core gameplay loop working and in turn trying to figure out what exactly it is I want from these kinds of games.

The dream of old school dungeon crawling has not been realised. I used dungeon turns, I tracked encumbrance, I used random encounters.

I've never actually played one of these older games for an extended period...
